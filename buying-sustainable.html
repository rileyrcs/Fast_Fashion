Buying sustainable

We know affordle fashion can be expensive as all get out so here are some sustainable fashion brands that won't brake the bank.

Pact Pact sells organic clothing for everyone, the brand is GOTS Certified organic, and their clothing is universally flattering. The price usually range from $15 to $100.
Girlfriend Collective

Girlfriend Collective uses recycled materials to create stunning activewear. And as a bonus the company practices ethical working conditions and fair wages.

Cuyana Make sure to check out Cuyana if you're a handbag and purse fiend like me. Cuyana uses recycled and upcylced materials to create stunning and elegantly styled leather bages for the modern woman. While Cuyana is more on the expensive side when it comes to sustainable fashion with prices ranging up to 300$, when it come to sustainable fashion fewer is better so make Cuyana your go to shop spot.
Gunas If Cuyana is a bit to expensive Gunas is a bit cheaper. This brand strives to create stelar bags using recyecled, upcycled, vegan, and vintage materials and are continuosly experimenting with new materials so try out these high fashion, cruelty-free handbags!
Veja

Calling all sneakerheads! If you've got that irresistible and uncontrollable urge for more street shoes that you can't seem to suppress but you're tired of of contributing to the fast fashion waste? Well I've got the brand for you! Veja is a street shoe brand based in France that uses eco friendly materials. They also give a portion of there proceeds to a charity that helps disadvantaged people reintegrate into society through work. They also offer worldwide shipping in packages made from recycled/recyclable cardboard. So if this isn't reason enough to get on board I don't know what is.

People Tree Just in case you need to be head-to-toe sustainable People Tree is a fair trade, GOTS certified organic cotton, PETA-certified brand that makes underwear and bras.
ABLE

ABLE is a brand that uses fair labor practices to create beautiful accessories.This business also creates opportunities for women to overcome adversity. So For my hardcore femenists out there wanting to empower your fellow women ABLE is the brand for you. Even if you are not you can still appreciate their beautiful pieces so go to ABLE for some social impact jewelry and accessories.

Even if none of these brands aren't a fit for you don't worry there are plentiy fish in the sea so you are sure to find the right brand for you.
