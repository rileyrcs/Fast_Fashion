The True Cost This documentary is an eye opening look into the fashion industry. As the title suggests it shows the viewer a glimpse of what the true cost of fast fashion is. The film takes a look at where clothing is made and the people who make it. It helps to humanize the fashion industry. We highly recommend you sit down and watch it when you can.
Good On You This website is a guide on all things sustainable fashion. They have a sustainability rating for all of your favorite brands and also have alternative options for those companies that aren't rated as highly, but are very popular. Please take some time to look at all Good On You has to offer, we promise it is worth it. In case you like it as much as we want you to know that there is also an app!
